# NOTICE

Some recipes here reproduce the kind of figure produced by established,
purpose-built tools. The intent is to show these layouts can be **composed from
plotlet's general primitives** — not to replace those tools. Credit to the
originals:

## Circular genome plots (`circle/`)

In the style of [Circos](http://circos.ca/) and
[pyCirclize](https://github.com/moshi4/pyCirclize). Some `circle/` recipes are
direct reproductions of pyCirclize's own examples.

## Annotated heatmaps (`heatmaps/`)

In the style of [ComplexHeatmap](https://github.com/jokergoo/ComplexHeatmap)
(R / Bioconductor) — its central-matrix + row/column dendrograms + annotation
tracks + shared legend layout, and the `ht1 + ht2` heatmap-list pattern.
